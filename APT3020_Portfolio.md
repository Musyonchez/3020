## Course Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Comprehend** the foundational principles and diverse categories of Knowledge-Based Systems (KBS)
2. **Master** fundamental techniques for representing knowledge in AI systems
3. **Apply and evaluate** different reasoning and inference mechanisms used in KBS
4. **Analyze** the architecture, development lifecycle, and tools associated with Expert Systems
5. **Utilize and compare** various problem-solving and search strategies within KBS
6. **Explore** advanced and contemporary topics in KBS, including machine learning integration and semantic web technologies

4. **References** - Sources used for research (APA 7 format)
